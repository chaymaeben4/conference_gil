Submission

  • Scientific papers should not exceed 6-10 pages.
  • Practical papers should not exceed 4-8 pages.
  • Discussion Papers should not exceed 2 pages.
  • All Scientific papers, Practical papers and Discussion Papers must follow the Springer template in order to be published.
  • All submissions should be written in English.
  • All submissions will be double-blind, peer-reviewed, and evaluated based on originality, technical and/or research content/depth, correctness, relevance to the conference, contributions, and readability.

Please submit your papers exclusively through the EasyChair Platform for a streamlined and efficient submission process.
