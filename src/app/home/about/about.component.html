The International Conference on Industrial and Logistics Systems (SIL’24)

SIL’24 is a multidisciplinary conference which brings together academics and industrials interested in industrial engineering topics. It thus aims the Design and Efficient Operation (Optimization, Improvement, Restructuring, Evaluation, etc.) of a broad scope of Industrial and Logistics Systems. SIL’24 mainly focuses on the four complementary views of the industrial and logistics system: the Operational view, the Informational view, the Technological view and the Decision-making view supported mainly by Industry 4.0 technologies.

SIL’24 is an edition that places particular emphasis on Cyber-Physical Production and Logistics Systems research. Related research works or professional applications would be more appreciated.
